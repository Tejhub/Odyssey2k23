Rules




* Event Name : DHWANITANTRA
* Musical band

* Rules :
* Bands performer must register within the mentioned date for registration.
* Demo must be given to the organising committee Demo performance may include both covered and/or original tracks.
* "The Bands" finalists (5-7) performers performer must play tracks.
* Each band performer must fill approximately 7 minutes but must not exceed 7 minutes.
* For every minute exceeding the 7 minutes limit, the band will be penalized 5 points from their total score.
* If after your band has demonstrated after the criteria explained above and fail to provide the same track, this violation will result in immediate disqualification.
* There is no limit to the number of bands performances who wish to apply for the competition and all submitted demos will be fairly appraised for shortlisting.
* Any distasteful behaviour, and or damage to the organiser Theatre will result in the automatic disqualification at the hands of management and judges.

* Team-Size : 5-7 members

* Registration Amount : Rs 500/- per team
