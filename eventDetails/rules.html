Rules




1. Every student should carry their respective college ids on both the days of the event.
2. No act of vulgarity will be entertained
3. No costumes and Slogans based on caste and language will be permitted in any of the events.
4. No flag to be used in any of the events.
5. Registration is mandatory to participate in any event.Receipt to be carried along.
6. The judges and event coordinators decision is final and binding.
7. Any changes in any of the processes will be communicated.
8. Students taking part in the events should register on the day of the fest at the registration committee. On spot entry is not obliged.
9. Participants should go through the event rules and adhere to the specifics.
10. Participation, props, music etc for certain events should be confirmed pre registration if required by contacting the event head.
11. A participant can participate only in one event during the simultaneous timings for events, a participant is not restricted to participation in any number of events as long as the event timings do not clash.
12. All the events will commence at the scheduled time; Punctuality will be appreciated.
13. Rules are subject to changes at the discretion of the Organizers.
14. Criteria for Overall championship include participation in maximum number of events as well as the total scores obtained in the competitions.
15. Participants to show the transaction id of the payment made for the event at the entrance. Hard copy of receipt and batch will be provided at the college on 22nd May 2023 at the registration desk.

VIOLATION OF ANY OF THESE RULES WILL LEAD TO DISQUALIFICATION
